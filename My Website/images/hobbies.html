Background

Hello, I'm Viviane and I was born in São Paulo (Brazil). I´ve lived in Campinas since my studies at Campinas University, and after my degree, I started to work with Distributed Control System. I love science, traveling and meeting people.

I have traveled to different countries on my vacation, experiencing new habits, customs, food, activities and landscapes. I visited Canada for the first time in 2016 and I fell in love with its multicultural and bilingual society, which I had never seen in another country. So, I decided to move to Vancouver due to the climate, hospitality and vibrant atmosphere, to study.

This will be my new journey, my new home !

Goals

I decided to study in Vancouver due to more opportunities in computer science area and excellent curriculum of the courses offered. In addition to improving my second language and my curriculum.

Living abroad, it will add an improvement in my ability to work and live in a multicultural team, expanding my knowledge and concepts, in a harmonious and beneficial way. A great leap in my academic and personal training.
